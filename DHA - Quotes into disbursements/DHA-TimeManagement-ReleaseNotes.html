DHA Time Management

What's New

A summary of the latest improvements and fixes to the DHA Time Management application.

New feature – something you can do now that you couldn't before
Fix – something that was broken and is now corrected

Recording Your Time

Record Time in the Future New

You can now log time for upcoming dates, not just today or the past. Useful for planning ahead or recording time before the end of a period.

Multi-Day Time Entries New

You can now create a single entry that spans multiple days, saving you from repeating the same entry over and over.

Hours & Minutes Display New

Time is now shown in hours and minutes (e.g. 1h 30m) instead of a decimal figure (e.g. 1.5). This makes it much easier to read at a glance.

Change Entry Category New

When editing a time entry you can now switch its category — for example, from Chargeable to Non-Chargeable, Speculative, or Hourly Rate — without having to delete and re-enter it.

Hourly Rate Option Added New

A new Hourly Rate category is now available when recording or editing time, covering work that is billed at an agreed hourly rate.

Monday Lock-Out Fix Fix

Previously, time entered on a Monday could sometimes be locked immediately and become impossible to edit, even though it had only just been saved. This has now been corrected.

Editing & Deleting Entries

Delete Within a 5-Day Window New

You can now delete time entries that fall within the last five working days. Previously the ability to remove entries was more restricted.

Auto-Updating Date Range New

The date range shown on screen will now update itself automatically. If you record time for yesterday, the view will expand to include yesterday so you can see that entry straight away without adjusting the filters manually.

Disbursements

Faster Disbursement Entry New

When you add a new time entry, that time entry's job details are now automatically carried across to the Disbursements section. This means you no longer have to search for the same job a second time. Additionally, you can right-click any time entry and choose Add Disbursement directly from the menu for even quicker access.

Searching & Finding Jobs

Recent Searches & Pinned Jobs New

The search panel now remembers your recent searches. You can also pin your most-used jobs so they appear above the search when you open the app — no need to search for frequently used projects at all.

Search by Client New

As well as searching by job number or name, you can now search by client name. This is particularly handy when you know the client but can't immediately recall the exact job reference.

Job Category Visible in List New

Jobs in the search results now show their category (such as Planning or Infrastructure), making it quicker to confirm you have selected the right job before recording time.

List No Longer Jumps on Selection Fix

When clicking on a job that had multiple time entries, the list would unexpectedly scroll down to the next job. This has been fixed — the list stays exactly where you clicked.

Layout & Usability

Resizeable Divider New

The divider between the job list and the time entries panel can now be dragged to resize each section. If you prefer to see more of your time entries or more of the job list, simply drag the divider to suit your preference.

Sign-In & Reliability

Automatic Sign-In Token Refresh New

The app will now try to silently renew your sign-in session in the background before it expires. In practice this means you should be asked to sign in much less frequently during a working day. Please note: there are limits to how long a session can remain active, so occasional sign-in prompts cannot be avoided entirely, but they should become far less common.

Anonymous Authentication Error Fix Fix

Some users were seeing an unexpected error message related to authentication on startup or after a period of inactivity. The underlying cause has been identified and resolved.
